FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to sign in to use the app?

You can use the basic features without signing in. Sign in with Apple ID or Google only if you want to sync your progress across multiple devices.

Will I be charged automatically when the trial ends?

No. The app does not require a subscription during the trial. After 7 days, you only subscribe yourself if you want to keep using premium features. You can cancel anytime from your Apple ID subscription settings.

What are the system requirements?

iPhone and iPad running iOS 18.0 or later. Apple Silicon Macs will also work via iPad compatibility mode.

Is it available for Android?

Currently in development. We will announce the release date on this site once it is set.

How is my personal data handled?

We collect only your email address, user ID, learning progress, and purchase history. We do not perform any tracking. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Do voice features work offline?

Voice input (when answering) is processed on-device, so it works offline. AI feedback and pronunciation analysis require an internet connection.
